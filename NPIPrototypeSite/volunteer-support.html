Ways To Donate

There are a variety of charitable giving opportunities available to alumni, families and friends. All gifts are gratefully accepted and individually acknowledged. Please contact our Development Office for assistance at 810-233-5340 x120, or contact us.

  • Annual Fund: The Annual Fund is foremost and vital New Paths Inc.’s charitable mission. These unrestricted funds are needed for growth and the long-term stability of the organization.
  • Capital Improvements: Donations to New Paths, Inc.’s Capital Improvements budget allows the organization to extend the lifespan of all facilities, which includes upgrades like new flooring, bathroom renovations, building remodels and interior decorations.
  • Sober House (SH): The Sober House works with individuals in need of substance use disorder services after experiencing an overdose or addiction related event as they begin their own journey toward addiction recovery. Funds are needed to grow our services and support groups.
  • Memorial Gifts: Memorial gifts are a wonderful way to celebrate someone’s sobriety or a special occasion, thank a friend or relative for their support, honor a special person in your life, or remember the life of a loved one.
  • Will or Bequest: This is the easiest and most frequently used method of deferred giving.
